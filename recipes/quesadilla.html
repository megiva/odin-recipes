Quesadilla Recipe

Description

A quesadilla is a Mexican dish consisting of a tortilla filled primarily with cheese, and sometimes meats, beans, vegetables, and spices, then cooked on a griddle or stove. It's a quick, versatile, and delicious meal or snack.

Ingredients

  • 2 flour tortillas
  • 1 cup shredded cheese
  • Optional fillings:
    • Cooked chicken, beef, or pork (1 cup, diced or shredded)
    • Sautéed vegetables (e.g., bell peppers, onions, mushrooms)
    • Black beans (1/2 cup, drained and rinsed)
    • Corn (1/2 cup)
    • Jalapeños (sliced, to taste)
  • Optional toppings:
    • Salsa
    • Guacamole
    • Sour cream
    • Fresh cilantro (chopped)

Steps

  1. Preheat: Head a non-stick skillet or griddle over medium heat.
  2. Prepare Tortilla: Place one tortilla in the skillet.
  3. Add Cheese: Sprinkle a generous amount of shredded cheese over the tortilla.
  4. Add Fillings: Add any additional fillings (e.g., cooked chicken, sautéed vegetables, beans) evenly over the cheese.
  5. Top with Another Tortilla: Place a second tortilla on top of the fillings.
  6. Cook: Cook until the bottom tortilla is golden brown and the cheese begins to melt (about 2-3 minutes).
  7. Flip: Carefully flip the quesadilla using a spatula. Cook the other side until it is also golden brown and the cheese is fully melted (another 2-3 minutes).
  8. Cut and Serve: Remove from the skillet, let it cool slightly, then cut into wedges and serve with salsa, guacamole, sour cream, or your favorite dip.
ENJOY!
